    Methodology & Sources

    These tools are designed to make public finance easier to understand and to support more informed civic decisions. The calculations use public budget sources and simplified allocation models, so results should be read as an informed estimate rather than a precise government statement.

    About Me

    I built this calculator project to help people visualize where their taxes may go in plain language. The goal is civic understanding: turn large budget categories into a clearer picture of public priorities.

    The calculator runs in your browser and uses simplified formulas based on public budget information, state spending patterns, and category descriptions.
