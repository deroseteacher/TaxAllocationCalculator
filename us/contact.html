Corrections and Feedback

TaxGal welcomes corrections, source suggestions, accessibility feedback, and clearer official budget mappings. Public finance data changes, and better sources can improve the calculator over time.

Government policy, budget, finance, audit, and program officials are especially welcome to contact TaxGal at hi@taxgal.org if they would like to help update the United States calculator with more accurate percentages based on current budget analysis.

For public officials and policy teams

The most useful contributions are specific and source-backed: a budget table, public accounts schedule, departmental plan, audited spending summary, or official explanation of how a category should be mapped.

Helpful correction details

  • The page name or URL where you saw the issue.
  • The spending category or sentence that needs review.
  • A link to an official source, budget, public accounts page, or data table when possible.
  • Any accessibility issue, such as contrast, keyboard navigation, or screen-reader clarity.
